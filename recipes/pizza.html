Pizza

What is a pizza?

A pizza is a flat, round, and thinly crispy dish made from tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and bread crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or pork or chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Gouda cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Marsala cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Emmental cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Fontina cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Gorgonzola cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Slice the beef, mozzarella, cheddar, and Parmesan cheeses into thin, round cubes.
  3. Place a large, square baking dish on a baking sheet.
  4. Spread the mozzarella cheese cubes evenly across the dish.
  5. Top the dish with the remaining cheeses, meats, and vegetables, following the pattern of a pizza crust.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden brown and the crust is crispy.
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes before slicing.
